FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before generating your .np domain cover letter.

Is .com.np domain registration really free?

Yes. Mercantile Communication Pvt. Ltd. registers .np domains free of cost, on a first-come-first-served basis. You only pay if you use a paid registrar/agency for handling the process, or for hosting

How long does .np domain approval take?

Typically 3–5 business days after you submit your cover letter and documents, though it can be faster depending on Mercantile's review queue.

What documents do I need for a .com.np cover letter?

For personal registration: citizenship or passport number, full name, and address. For business registration: company registration certificate, PAN/VAT number, and authorized representative details. Both need your desired domain name and two nameservers.

Can foreigners register a .com.np domain?

Foreign individuals can register with a Non-Resident Nepali (NRN) ID card or valid residence visa. Foreign companies need an official presence in Nepal — as a joint venture, partner, agent, or licensee.

Does my domain name have to match my business or personal name?

Yes — the requested domain name must exactly match, be an abbreviation or acronym of, or be closely connected to the applicant's name or trademark. Mercantile can reject requests where the connection isn't clear.

What's the difference between .com.np, .org.np, and .name.np?

.com.np is for businesses and general use, .org.np is for non-profits and organizations, and .name.np is for individuals registering a personal domain under their own name.

Do I need a nameserver before I register my .com.np domain?

Yes — you need to provide primary and secondary nameservers (NS1 and NS2) as part of your application. These usually come from your hosting provider.

Is this cover letter generator free to use?

Yes, it's completely free with no signup required.

Is my personal information stored when I use this tool?

No — your information is used only to generate your PDF and isn't stored on our servers.

What format does Mercantile accept for the cover letter?

A formal letter addressed to the Hostmaster at Mercantile Communication Pvt. Ltd., including applicant details, the requested domain, nameservers, and a signature — this tool generates that exact format automatically.
