Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

The short version

No login or account needed to use the generator.

We only keep your name, email, and requested domain.

Citizenship numbers, addresses, and stamps are never saved.

We never sell or share your information with anyone.

1

What We Collect

When you generate a cover letter, we log a small amount of information for support and record-keeping purposes:

  • Your name (or company name, for business applications)
  • Your email address
  • The domain name and extension you requested
  • The date and time of your submission
2

What We Don't Store

We do not save the following to our database, even though you enter it into the form to generate your letter:

  • Citizenship or ID numbers
  • Company registration numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Business stamps or uploaded images
  • The full text of your generated cover letter

This information is used only in your browser session to generate your PDF or JPEG file, and is discarded once your session ends.

3

How Your Letter Is Generated

Your form details are used temporarily, on our server, to render your cover letter as a downloadable PDF or JPEG. This happens in real time when you click Generate, and the underlying document data is not written to permanent storage — only the summary fields listed above are retained.

4

No Login Required

You don't need to create an account to use this tool. We don't track you across visits with a persistent profile, and there's no password or personal dashboard tied to your submissions.

5

Cookies & Sessions

We use a temporary session cookie so your generated letter can be downloaded after you submit the form. This session data is cleared automatically and is not used for tracking or advertising purposes.

6

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. The name, email, and domain details we retain are used solely to respond to support requests and understand tool usage — nothing more.

7

Your Submission to Mercantile

Once you download your cover letter, submitting it to Mercantile Communications Pvt. Ltd. for domain registration is between you and Mercantile. We are not affiliated with Mercantile and don't have visibility into your actual registration status.

8

Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change, we'll update this page and revise the "last updated" date above.
